Kingdom of Kongo

Disclaimer: This was created for a school project and time travel is just a fictitious technology. We do not offer any real services that enable any sort of time travel.


 Above Kingdom of Kongo 1600 BCE

   Around 1300 BCE, several small Kingdoms merged and became the Kingdom of Kongo. Wealth blessed the kingdom which allowed them to have a powerful millitary to keep them safe. However, within a century the Porteguese would come to their kingdom and begin slave trade. In the long run, this made the slave trade route overshadow the two regions relationships and led to their downfall due to lack of manpower.

   With a bit of history aside, now we can talk about our first attraction, the Cathedral of the Holy Savior. This attraction is a Cathedral that was built in 1892 and as of now it is the oldest standing Cathedral in South Africa. And to be honest, this Cathedral is quite the sight being able to look back at all the history the building itself will tell. But what is even better, is seeing it during its glory days thanks to the modern wonder of time travel.


Above Ruins of the Cathedral of the Holy Savior 

   However, we have one more untradional attraction for you to look at. Afonso the First, the sixth king of Kongo, was quite the powerful leader. He pushed for Catholic Christianity as part of Kongo and provided many improvements to the security and protections for Kongo. In 1512, he ensured the relations with Portugal were good by extending them. However, in 1526 he discovered that Portugal was purchasing illegally enslaved people and therefore created a commitee to help prevent people from being illegally enslaved.

How the Trip Works

   At first, you and the tour operator will go inside our new 2022 Mustang Time Machine. However, you are only allowed to go on the trip assuming you sign all of our documents to ensure you will not manipulate any previous events and will attempt to blend in. You will be given clothes that were suitable at each time as well to look more native. First for the Kingdom of Kongo trip is the 1892 Cathedral of the Holy Savior. This allows you to see the monumental structure in its glory days. After that, you will go back to 1515 to visit Alfonso the First. Plus, if things go awry during the trip, we can use the Mustang and show our advanced technology while claiming to be gods. Overall, this trip is a blast from the literal past!
